SMART BUZI ANALYZER
PROJECT INSIGHTS FOR HACKATHON DESCRIPTION :
AI-DRIVEN SMART BUZI ANALYZER is an AI-powered autonomous business feasibility agent that combines machine learning model, geospatial intelligence, and LLMs to predict business success rate and failure rate, risks, and profitability estimation before investment.
	So many entrepreneurs starts surveying in surrounding locations about 6 to 8 months for the total analysis and our application will reduces the time for surveying.

MAIN PROBLEM :
	Nowadays around 20%-22% of new businesses fail within the first year.
	Around 50% of new businesses fail within the 5 years and in long term.
	Some startups are up to 90% eventually fail in long term.
	Nowadays entrepreneur dream confronts as a harsh reality and they are making critical decisions in dark.
CAUSES : 
	Lack of awareness about the location suitability, Population density, foot traffic and about surrounding competitors the people who are new for businesses.
	Lack of awareness about that the when the investment will be returns and that timeline of the return investment(in months or years) which is called Break-Even.
	Lack of awareness about the risk analysis of the business that they want to start.
WHAT MY APPLICATION DOES :
	Business location suitability Analysis
	Competition and market saturation 
	Financial feasibility like investment and monthly Expenses and break-even time period
	Risk factors analysis that are causing business failure
	Probability of success rate vs failure rate
MY AI AGENT ESTIMATES :
	Initial setup cost
	Monthly operational expenses
	Rent, salaries, utilities, marketing
	Break-even period
	Required monthly revenue for profitability
	Suggestions to success there business at the specific location like area failure trends, detects and shows the competitors in the surrounding areas where they are planning to start their business.
TARGET AUDIENCE AND END USERS:
	 Entrepreneurs & startups
	 Small business owners
	 Franchise planners
	 Urban planners & investors
	 MSME decision-makers
THE MAIN MOTO OF THE PROJECT :
The main aim of this project is to help people make informed business decisions using artificial intelligence instead of assumptions. The system analyses location, competition, costs, and risks to predict the success or failure of a business idea before investment.
The improving success through our application is 40% - 45% is a huge real impact and it will helps to improve economy of our country.
